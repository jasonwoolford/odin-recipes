Cast Iron Chicken Fajitas

Description

A fantastic dish both savory and wholesome. Marinated chicken cooked with all the fajita regulars: bell peppers, onion, and lime juice.

Ingredients

    Marinade:
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast
    Vegetables:
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 large red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 large green bell pepper, sliced

Steps

  1. Combine marinade ingredients in a bowl or ziploc bag, and put the combined ingredients in the fridge for a minimum of 4 hours. This marinade can be left in the fridge overnight if that is preferred.
  2. Heat a cast iron skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add chicken breast to the heated skillet (reserving the marinade) and cook until brown. (6-8 minutes)
  4. Remove chicken from skillet, slice, and set aside.
  5. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in the same skillet and add onion, bell peppers, and reserved marinade. Cook until vegetables are softened and tender. (5-7 minutes)
  6. After vegetables have softened, add the sliced chicken back into the skillet with all the other ingredeints, tossing the meat and vegetables in the marinade for another 2 to 4 minutes.
  7. Serve warm!
